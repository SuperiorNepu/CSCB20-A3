Late Submissions

Your work will be penalized by 10% for each day (up to 3 days) of the late submission unless a valid reason for the late submission is provided.

Remark Requests

If a piece of work has been mismarked or if you believe the rubric used to evaluate the work is not appropiate, you may request a remark within 48 hours. Be prepared for the entire work to be re-evaluated.

Partner Work

Each group can have up to 3 people. Once you begin working on an assignment, you may not dissolve your partnership without my permission. All team members will receive the same mark.

Academic Integrity

All the work you submit must be your own and your work must not be submitted by someone else. Plagarism is academic fraud and is taken seriously. The department uses software that compares programs for evidence of similar code. Please read the rules and regulations from the UofT Calendar (especially the Code of Behaviour on Academic Matters)
